The Report That Changes Behavior

No jargon, no panic — just clear actions Priya can take today

NutriGuard — Daily Report
📊 Today's Nutrition Summary
⚠ Needs Attention
• Tea at 8:30 AM was within 90 min of your iron tablet — tannins can reduce absorption by 60–70%
• Dahi at lunch provides calcium that competes with iron absorption
• Breakfast was low in protein
💡 Suggestions
• Move tea to 10 AM — keep 2 hours from iron
• Add sprouts, paneer, or tofu for morning protein
• Consider having dahi at dinner instead
🛡️ Safety
• Follow your doctor's guidance on iron supplement timing and dosage

Report Design Principles

🎯
Actionable
"Move tea to 10 AM" — not "consider modifying intake patterns"
🤝
Non-alarming
Practical nutrition guidance, never diagnoses or prescriptions
🛡️
Safety-first
Always defers to clinician; includes safety note when relevant
📅
Day-level view
Analyzes meals in context of the full day, not in isolation
👍
Feedback loop
Users rate report quality — thumbs up/down per section improves AI over time
"No jargon, no panic. Just: 'Move your tea to 10 AM. Add sprouts for protein. Follow your doctor's advice on iron timing.'"
